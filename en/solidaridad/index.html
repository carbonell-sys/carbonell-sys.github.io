LIFE FOR AFRICA programme

The LIFE FOR AFRICA programme provides support to pregnant women, newborns and children in sub-Saharan Africa, one of the regions with the highest maternal and neonatal mortality in the world.

Convinced that education is essential to sustainable healthcare, ten years ago we launched the project to build a teaching maternity and neonatal hospital within a university campus in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Following years of dedication and generous donations, the hospital opened in October 2017.
