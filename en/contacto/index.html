How to get here

The clinic is located at Carrer d'Anglí, 59, in the Sarrià-Sant Gervasi district of Barcelona. It is well connected by public transport and has nearby parking options.

Public transport

  • Metro: Lines L3 (Lesseps) or L7 (Av. Tibidabo)
  • Bus: Routes 22, 27, 60, 73, 123
  • FGC: Av. Tibidabo

By car

Parking is available nearby. We recommend checking the surrounding streets for the best options.
